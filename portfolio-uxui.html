Mobile App Design #1

Well Fusion- Mental Health App

Well Fusion is a mobile app designed to help users manage their mental health and well-being. The app offers a range of features, including mood tracking, guided meditation, and cognitive behavioral therapy exercises. The design focuses on creating a calming and supportive environment for users, with a clean and intuitive interface that makes it easy to navigate and use the app's features.

Mobile App Design #2

Stampede Produce- Farmer's Market App

Stampede Produce is a mobile app designed to connect local farmers with consumers in the Calgary area. The app allows users to browse and purchase fresh produce directly from local farmers, as well as learn about the farmers and their practices. The design focuses on creating a user-friendly and visually appealing interface that highlights the quality and variety of local produce available through the app.
